Numbered Discourses 5.173

18. A Lay Follower

Hell

“Mendicants, a lay follower with five qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

What five?

They kill living creatures, steal, commit sexual misconduct, lie, and consume beer, wine, and liquor intoxicants.

A lay follower with these five qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

A lay follower with five qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.

What five?

They don’t kill living creatures, steal, commit sexual misconduct, lie, or consume beer, wine, and liquor intoxicants.

A lay follower with these five qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.”
