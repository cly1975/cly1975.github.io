Numbered Discourses 10.214

21. The Body Born of Deeds

A Laywoman

“A laywoman with ten qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

What ten?

She kills living creatures. …

She has wrong view. …

A laywoman with these ten qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

A laywoman with ten qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.

What ten?

She doesn’t kill living creatures. …

She has right view. …

A laywoman with these ten qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.”
