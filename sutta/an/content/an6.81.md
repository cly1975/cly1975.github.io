Numbered Discourses 6.81

8. Perfection

Hell (1st)

“Mendicants, someone with six qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

What six?

They kill living creatures, steal, commit sexual misconduct, and lie. And they have corrupt wishes and wrong view.

Someone with these six qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

Someone with six qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.

What six?

They don’t kill living creatures, steal, commit sexual misconduct, or lie. And they have few desires and right view.

Someone with these six qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.”
