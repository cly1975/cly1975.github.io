Linked Discourses 23.21

2. About Māra (2nd)

Liable To Originate

At Sāvatthī.

Seated to one side, Venerable Rādha said to the Buddha:

“Sir, they speak of a ‘liability to originate’.

What has a liability to originate?”

“Rādha, form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness have a liability to originate.

Seeing this …

They understand: ‘… there is nothing further for this place.’”
