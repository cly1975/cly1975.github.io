14. Burning Chaff

Observing Not-Self

At Sāvatthī.

“Mendicants, when a gentleman has gone forth out of faith, this is what’s in line with the teaching.

They should meditate observing not-self in form,

feeling,

perception,

choices,

and consciousness. …

They’re freed from suffering, I say.”
