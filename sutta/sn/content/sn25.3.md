Linked Discourses 25.3

1. The Eye

Consciousness

At Sāvatthī.

“Mendicants, eye consciousness is impermanent, decaying, and perishing.

Ear consciousness,

nose consciousness,

tongue consciousness,

body consciousness,

and mind consciousness are impermanent, decaying, and perishing.

Someone who has faith and confidence in these teachings is called a follower by faith. …”
