Linked Discourses 45.14

2. Meditation

Arising (1st)

At Sāvatthī.

“Mendicants, these eight things don’t arise to be developed and cultivated except when a Realized One, a perfected one, a fully awakened Buddha has appeared.

What eight?

They are: right view, right purpose, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right immersion.

These eight things don’t arise to be developed and cultivated except when a Realized One, a perfected one, a fully awakened Buddha has appeared.”
