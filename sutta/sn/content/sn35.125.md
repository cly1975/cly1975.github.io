13. Householders

In the Land of the Vajjis

At one time the Buddha was staying in the land of the Vajjis at Elephant Village.

Then the householder Uggata of Elephant Village went up to the Buddha, sat down to one side, and said to him:

“What is the cause, sir, what is the reason why some sentient beings are not fully extinguished in this very life?
